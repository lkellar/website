DeweyCam

I lived on Dewey Ave in Ann Arbor from August 2023 until May 2026. In the spring of 2024, after finals, I had an old Raspberry Pi laying around, so I bought a camera module and taped it to my bedroom window.

The live feed from that camera used to be on this page, but I don't know if the new tenants would appreciate someone else's camera in their room.

Every day at noon, it snapped a photo. You can see one below.
